About

Ask Toscanini! is a first-of-a-kind deep search engine for music scores, developed at Virginia Tech. It aims to make finding music scores easier for musicians and musicologists by allowing for custom search queries.

Developer: Arman Bahraini (arman1 at vt.edu)
Supervisor: Dr. Tilevich (tilevich at cs.vt.edu)

Instructions

Suppose you want pieces with flute that plays in range D4 G#6 and viola in C3 E5...

  1. Enter an instrument range query in the search box below as "flute D4 G#6 and viola C3 E5" (without quotes)
  2. Hit the "Toscanini Search" button and we'll show you our matching scores as PDF or MusicXML !
Supported Queries
  • -See all scores we have by leaving the search box empty
  • -Query for a single composer, ex: "bach"
  • -Query for tempo between range, ex: "tempo 40 130", however if a piece has no written tempo it will pass for any tempo
  • -Query for a single key signature, ex: "key Bb"
  • -Query for a single time signature, ex: "ts 2 4"
  • -Putting it all together: "Beethoven and tempo 80 170 and flute D4 G#6 and trumpet F#3 A5 and key Bb and ts 2 4"
Side Notes
  • -This engine is not case sensitive
  • -Queries are checked for name inclusion, so querying for "trumpet F3 D5" can give you scores with "Trumpets in C" assuming the range is appropriate
